Lecturer Profile

I am a UK-based Lecturer and Module Leader with extensive experience teaching Economics, Business, Statistics, and Applied Economics across UK, Australian, and Sri Lankan higher education systems. I currently deliver and lead undergraduate modules aligned with UK HEQF standards, with strong expertise in assessment design, moderation, quality assurance, and student-centred learning.

My teaching practice is informed by research and professional scholarship, with clear alignment to the UK Professional Standards Framework (UKPSF). I bring a strong commitment to inclusive pedagogy, equality and diversity, academic integrity, and continuous professional development. Alongside teaching, I maintain an active research profile in applied economics, social policy, and quantitative analysis, with peer-reviewed publications and international conference presentations.
